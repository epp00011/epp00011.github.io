Notes

5 HTML tags

Heading Tags
These are headings for the page, the browser styles them differently depending on the number you start with. The biggest is h1 which is for the most important heading and it goes all the way to h6.
Comment Tag
This tag is to give yourself or other coders and partners in a group a note that the people browsing the site cannot see.
Emphasis Tag
This tag is to add emphasis to slightly more important text. It italicized the text to show the emphasis of the word or words.
Quote Tag
This tag is when you embed a small quote in your text. It shows quotation marks around the words you put the tag around.
Unordered List Tag
This is when you wish to have a list of items with no specific order to them. You put the ul tag for the unordered list, then list your items in separate li tags.
